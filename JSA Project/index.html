Batman is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, and first appeared in Detective Comics #27. Wikipedia - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Batman_(serial).

Batman has gone through many changes over the years with two main turning points. Transition one happened in 1966 with the release of the TV series, Batman, featuring Adam West. The second transition happened in 2008 with the release of the Batman movie, "The Dark Knight".
